About Doctor Wallet

What is Doctor Wallet?

Doctor Wallet is a comprehensive digital medical management system designed to streamline healthcare operations. It bridges the gap between healthcare providers and patients through secure, efficient digital tools.

The system consists of two integrated applications that work together to provide a complete healthcare management solution for clinics of all sizes.

What is Laboratory Portal?

The Laboratory Portal is a specialized module designed for medical laboratories to upload, manage, and deliver lab reports directly to patients and doctors. It streamlines the entire lab report workflow with secure digital delivery.

Laboratory staff can efficiently manage test results, generate reports, and send automated notifications to patients when their reports are ready for viewing.
